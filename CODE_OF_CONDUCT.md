# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want Baker to be a constructive project for users, contributors, and operators.

Please:

- be respectful and patient
- assume good intent, then ask clarifying questions
- keep feedback specific, technical, and actionable
- make room for contributors using either English or Simplified Chinese

## Unacceptable Behavior

This project does not accept:

- harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- doxxing or posting someone else's private information
- deliberately hostile, discriminatory, or demeaning language
- posting secrets, credentials, or exploit details in public threads

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that violates these rules and may restrict repeat offenders from participating.

## Reporting

If conduct itself is the issue, open a minimal maintainer-contact issue without reposting private information.
